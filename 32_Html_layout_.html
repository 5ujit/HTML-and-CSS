Amaravati

Amaravati is the capital of the Indian state of Andhra pardesh.It is located at right bank of the river Krishna in Guntur district.

Amaravathi Village ,a historical and Religious Hub:

Older Than Time: Predating even Dharanikota, this village was a flourishing Buddhist center during the Mauryan Empire. The Amaravati Maha Stupa, one of the earliest and largest brick stupas in India, stands as a testament to its rich past
